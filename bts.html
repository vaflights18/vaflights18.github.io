#BeyondTheScoreboard

Because it's not just about basketball, it’s beyond the scoreboard.


We go beyond the scoreboard by volunteering around our community because we value teamwork and commmunity.

Virginia Flights had the opportunity to volunteer at JCOC with their food pantry. The girls and everyone involved enjoyed helping and being a part of this community service event. It was a huge success as it was our first volunteer event and can’t wait to do more in the future.

In addition to the community service event, the Virginia Flights ladies paid it forward with a random act of kindness. Filling the tank of a random person in the community!
